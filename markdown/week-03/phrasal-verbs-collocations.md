## Collocations — exam traps

### MAKE
make a decision, make a mistake, make progress, make an effort, make money, make sure

**Not:** make homework → **do** homework

### TAKE
take a photo, take a break, take an exam, take place, take care, take part

### DO
do research, do your best, do harm, do business

### HAVE
have a rest, have fun, have trouble, have an impact on

### GET
get in touch, get rid of, get married, get permission

### Adjective + noun
heavy rain, strong coffee, sharp contrast, wide range, key role, vital importance

## Practice — choose A, B, C, or D

1. You should ______ a break every hour when studying.
   - A) make  B) do  C) take  D) have

2. Researchers ______ a discovery last year.
   - A) did  B) made  C) took  D) had

3. The conference will ______ place in Lviv.
   - A) make  B) take  C) do  D) give

4. I need to ______ up the word «collocation».
   - A) look  B) find  C) give  D) take

5. She ______ up smoking in 2023.
   - A) gave  B) took  C) put  D) looked

6. Please ______ in the application form online.
   - A) fill  B) write  C) put  D) take

7. Success often depends ______ regular practice.
   - A) in  B) on  C) at  D) for

8. He was ______ forward to meeting his supervisor.
   - A) looking  B) seeing  C) waiting  D) going

**Key:** 1-C  2-B  3-B  4-A  5-A  6-A  7-B  8-A
