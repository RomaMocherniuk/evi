## Relative Clauses

| Pronoun | Use |
|---------|-----|
| who | people |
| which | things |
| that | people/things (defining) |
| where | places |
| whose | possession |

**Defining** (no commas): The book **that** I read was useful.  
**Non-defining** (commas): My supervisor, **who** lives in Berlin, visits monthly.

### Gaps
1. The student ______ applied earliest received a scholarship. (who/which/where)
2. This is the library ______ I studied last year.
3. Dr Lee, ______ research is famous, will lecture tomorrow.

**Key:** 1-who  2-where  3-whose

## Gerund vs Infinitive

| Verb + -ing | Verb + to infinitive |
|-------------|----------------------|
| enjoy, finish, mind, suggest, avoid | want, hope, decide, plan, agree, refuse |
| stop **doing** (quit) | stop **to do** (pause in order to) |
| remember **doing** (past memory) | remember **to do** (future duty) |

### Gaps
4. She avoided ______ (make) the same mistake.
5. They decided ______ (apply) abroad.
6. I remember ______ (meet) him at the conference last year.

**Key:** 4-making  5-to apply  6-meeting

## Reported Speech (basics)

**Backshift:** Present → Past, will → would, can → could

- «I **am** busy.» → She said she **was** busy.
- «I **will** help.» → He said he **would** help.

### Gaps
7. She told me she ______ (not have) time yesterday.
8. He said he ______ (finish) the essay the next day.

**Key:** 7-didn't have  8-would finish

## Second Conditional (preview for Week 4)

**If + Past Simple, would + infinitive** (unreal present/future)

If I **had** more time, I **would read** more academic articles.
