## Step 2 — Drill bank (pick by priority)

### A. Articles (if weak)

Fill in a / an / the / —

1. She is ______ university student studying ______ economics.
2. ______ Thames flows through London.
3. I need ______ advice about ______ scholarship application.
4. We met at ______ conference in ______ Netherlands last year.

**Key:** 1-a/—  2-The  3-—/the  4-a/the

### B. Phrasal verbs (if weak)

1. I need to ______ (find out) the registration deadline.
2. She ______ (give up) her job to study full-time.
3. We ______ (look forward to) the results.
4. He ______ (run out) of time during the mock.

**Key:** find out; gave up; are looking forward to; ran out

### C. Reading paraphrase (if weak)

**Mini text:** «The policy reduced traffic in the city centre.»

Which option matches?
- A) More cars entered the centre.
- B) Fewer vehicles drove downtown.
- C) The policy banned all buses.
- D) Traffic increased everywhere.

**Answer:** B (paraphrase drill — same word «traffic» NOT in correct answer as identical phrase)

### D. Present Perfect vs Past Simple (if weak)

See [../week-02/grammar-present-perfect.md](../week-02/grammar-present-perfect.md) — repeat Drill 1–2
