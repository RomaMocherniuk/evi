# A1–A2 Test 1 — EVI Format Practice

**Level:** A1–A2 | **Total:** 30 questions | **Time:** 45 minutes | **No dictionary**

> **Format:** Same three-task structure as the real ЄВІ English block.
> Task 1 (6 pts) + Task 2 (5 pts) + Task 3 (19 pts)

## Task 1 — Matching (Questions 1–6)

Read texts 1–6. Match each text with the most suitable heading (A–H).
There are **two extra headings** you do not need.

**A)** A place to buy things for your home

**B)** Information about a sports event

**C)** A place where children can learn something new

**D)** An advertisement for a job

**E)** A description of a weekend activity for families

**F)** Information about a change to public transport

**G)** An announcement about the weather

**H)** A notice about changes to opening hours

---

**1.** The number 12 bus will not run on Sunday 3 November. Passengers can take bus number 14 instead. The service will return to normal on Monday morning.

**2.** Summer Art Camp is open to children aged 6 to 12. This year, kids will paint, make ceramics, and learn basic photography. Places are limited, so register online before 30 June.

**3.** Strong winds and heavy rain are expected tomorrow morning. Temperatures will be between 8 and 12 degrees. People should drive carefully and stay inside if possible.

**4.** Sunrise Kitchen needs a part-time assistant from Monday to Friday, 8 a.m. to 1 p.m. You do not need experience — full training is provided. Please call 044 123 456 or visit us in person.

**5.** Come to City Furniture for our spring sale this weekend! All sofas, chairs, and tables are 30% cheaper than usual. We are open on Saturday and Sunday from 10 a.m. to 7 p.m.

**6.** Join us at City Park on Saturday for a 5-kilometre fun run for all ages! Registration starts at 8:30 a.m. Children under 10 take part for free.

## Task 2 — Multiple Choice (Questions 7–11)

### Text: Anna's First Week at University

Anna started university last month. It is very different from school. The campus is large, with many buildings, a big library, and a sports centre. Anna's room is in a student dormitory not far from the main lecture hall.

In the first week, she met her new classmates. Some of them came from small towns, and others came from big cities. Anna was a little shy at first, but soon she felt comfortable. She found a friend called Mia who also liked painting.

Every morning, Anna goes to lectures from nine to twelve. In the afternoon, she often studies in the library or does homework in her room. She has dinner in the university canteen at six o'clock. The food is simple but cheap.

**7.** How is university different from school, according to the text?
- A) It is much smaller and quieter.
- B) It is larger and has more buildings.
- C) It has no library or sports centre.
- D) It closes at five o'clock every day.

**8.** Where does Anna sleep?
- A) At her parents' house.
- B) In a hotel near the campus.
- C) In a student dormitory.
- D) In the university library.

**9.** How did Anna feel during her first week?
- A) Happy and confident from the start.
- B) Angry because the food was bad.
- C) A little shy at first.
- D) Very tired every single day.

**10.** What does Anna's new friend Mia like doing?
- A) Playing sports.
- B) Cooking new dishes.
- C) Painting.
- D) Listening to music.

**11.** What does Anna usually do in the afternoon?
- A) Goes shopping in the city centre.
- B) Studies or does her homework.
- C) Watches TV in the common room.
- D) Takes a long nap in her room.

## Task 3 — Gapped Text (Questions 12–30)

Read the email. Choose the best word (A, B, C, or D) for each gap.

---

Hi Mum,

How (12)______ you? I (13)______ you this letter because I want to tell you about my new life here.

My room is small (14)______ it is very comfortable. There (15)______ a desk, a bed, and a small wardrobe. I keep my books on the desk (16)______ my pens and notebooks in the wardrobe.

Every day, I (17)______ up at half past seven. I (18)______ a quick shower and then I go to the canteen (19)______ breakfast. The food is not fantastic, (20)______ it is OK.

My classes start (21)______ nine in the morning. The teachers here (22)______ much faster than my teachers at school! I need to take notes very quickly. Last Tuesday, I (23)______ a test in maths. It (24)______ not very difficult, (25)______ I still made a few mistakes.

I have already (26)______ some new friends. My favourite is Mia. She (27)______ from Lviv and she likes painting, just (28)______ me! We went to (29)______ art exhibition together last Friday. It was absolutely wonderful.

I miss you very much. Please say hello to Dad (30)______ my sister.

Love, Anna
