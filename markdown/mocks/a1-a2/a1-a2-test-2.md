**Level:** A1–A2 | **Total:** 30 questions | **Time:** 45 minutes | **No dictionary**

> **Format:** Same three-task structure as the real ЄВІ English block.

## Task 1 — Matching (Questions 1–6)

Match each situation (1–6) with the most suitable notice (A–H).
There are **two extra notices** you do not need.

**A)** No entry without a ticket.

**B)** Showers are available in Room 12.

**C)** Bags must go through the security scanner.

**D)** Check in between 2 p.m. and 10 p.m.

**E)** Swimming pool open from 7 a.m. daily.

**F)** Last train to the city centre at 11:30 p.m.

**G)** Café on the third floor — hot drinks and snacks.

**H)** Push the red button for emergency help.

---

**1.** You want to swim early in the morning before work.

**2.** You are arriving at your hotel late in the evening and want to know if you can still check in.

**3.** You are at the airport and need to send your suitcase through the check-in process.

**4.** You are hungry and want to find a place to eat inside the building.

**5.** You need to catch the last train home tonight.

**6.** You want to enter a concert but you forgot to buy a ticket.

## Task 2 — Multiple Choice (Questions 7–11)

### Text: Tom's First Job

Tom started his first job last month. He works at a coffee shop in the town centre. Every day, he gets up at six o'clock and arrives at work at seven. The coffee shop opens at half past seven.

Tom's favourite part of the job is talking to customers. They are usually friendly and polite. Sometimes people leave a small tip on the table, and Tom uses the money to buy his lunch.

Tom earns money every week. He is saving to buy a new laptop for university. His parents are very proud of him because he is responsible and hard-working.

**7.** When did Tom start his job?
- A) Last year.
- B) Last summer.
- C) Last month.
- D) Last week.

**8.** What time does the coffee shop open?
- A) At six o'clock.
- B) At half past seven.
- C) At seven o'clock.
- D) At eight o'clock.

**9.** What does Tom like most about his job?
- A) Making different types of coffee.
- B) Counting the money at the end of the day.
- C) Cleaning the tables and chairs.
- D) Talking to customers.

**10.** What is Tom saving money for?
- A) To buy a new car.
- B) To travel to another country.
- C) To buy a laptop for university.
- D) To pay his rent.

**11.** How do Tom's parents feel about him?
- A) Worried, because the job is difficult.
- B) Proud, because he is responsible.
- C) Surprised, because he found a job.
- D) Sad, because he works long hours.
