## Teacher Notes

- Main grammar tested: **past perfect**, **could/couldn't**, **comparatives**, **phrasal verbs** (sign up), **fixed expressions** (more and more, there will be times, better than)
- Key collocations: "thinking about + gerund", "surround yourself with", "keep going", "faster than you think"
- Common A2 errors: using "more good" instead of "better", forgetting phrasal verb particles
