# Gapped Text — Basics (5 gaps, Week 1)

**Format:** Choose A, B, C, or D for each gap.  
**Strategy:** Grammar or lexis? → Insert → Re-read sentence.

## Text — Student Life

Many students find that the first year of master's studies is (1)______ than they expected. They must read long academic texts in English and write summaries every week. At first, this can feel overwhelming, but regular practice helps.

Successful learners often create a fixed schedule. They (2)______ aside two hours each evening for reading and note-taking. They also use online dictionaries, but they try to guess meaning from context (3)______ looking up every unknown word.

Group work is another important skill. Students who (4)______ part in study groups usually understand difficult topics faster. Finally, it is wise to ask professors for feedback early, (5)______ than waiting until the final exam.

| Gap | A | B | C | D |
|-----|---|---|---|---|
| (1) | hard | harder | hardest | more hard |
| (2) | set | sets | setting | have set |
| (3) | before | after | instead of | because |
| (4) | take | takes | took | taking |
| (5) | rather | more | less | few |

## Text 2 — Travel (5 gaps)

Last summer, Maria decided to (1)______ a trip to Scotland. She had never travelled alone before, so she felt quite nervous. However, she found a cheap hostel online and booked a ticket.

The journey (2)______ longer than she planned because of a train delay. When she finally arrived, the weather was cold and rainy, but the landscape was beautiful. Maria spent three days hiking and visiting small museums.

She (3)______ many photos and wrote a blog about her experience. Her friends said they would like to (4)______ her example next year. Maria realised that travelling alone had made her more confident and independent. She is already saving money for her next adventure, (5)______ she hopes will be in Ireland.

| Gap | A | B | C | D |
|-----|---|---|---|---|
| (1) | do | make | take | have |
| (2) | takes | took | has taken | was taking |
| (3) | made | did | took | got |
| (4) | follow | agree | go | listen |
| (5) | who | which | where | whose |
