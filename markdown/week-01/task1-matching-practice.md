# Task 1 — Matching Practice (формат ЄВІ)

**Instructions for student:** Read texts 1–6. Match headings A–H to texts. Two headings are extra.  
**Time:** 10 minutes per set.

---

## Strategy reminder

1. Read headings A–H — underline key words
2. Skim each text 1–6 — find **main idea** (not details)
3. Eliminate 2 extra headings
4. Double-check: no duplicate matches

---

## Practice Set A

**Headings:**
- A) A place to improve your language skills
- B) Warning about dangerous weather
- C) How to eat more healthily
- D) Join a community sports event
- E) Learn about local history
- F) Discounts on winter clothing
- G) Help for students with exam stress
- H) New rules for city transport

**Text 1**  
The City Library has opened a free conversation club every Thursday at 6 p.m. Native speakers volunteer to talk with learners. No registration is required.

**Text 2**  
The weather service expects strong winds and heavy rain tonight. Residents should stay indoors and avoid unnecessary travel. Emergency services are on alert.

**Text 3**  
Dr Smith recommends eating more vegetables and drinking water instead of sugary drinks. Small changes can improve your energy and help you sleep better.

**Text 4**  
The annual Fun Run will take place in Central Park on Sunday. All ages are welcome. Money raised will support local schools.

**Text 5**  
Guided tours of the Old Town begin at the museum gate. Visitors will hear stories about famous people who lived there two hundred years ago.

**Text 6**  
The Student Support Centre offers free counselling and study skills workshops before exam season. Appointments can be booked online.

## Common traps (discuss with student)

- **Same word trap:** heading says «library» but text says «conversation club» → still A (language skills)
- **Detail trap:** «Thursday 6 p.m.» is detail; main idea = free language practice
- **Visual guessing:** if demo uses pictures — read ALL words on image
