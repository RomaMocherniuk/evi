## Task 1 — Matching

Match headings A–H to texts 1–6. Two are extra.

**A)** Free online courses for beginners  
**B)** Recycling household waste  
**C)** Evening concerts in the park  
**D)** Medical check-ups for adults  
**E)** Cheap flights to Europe  
**F)** Training for marathon runners  
**G)** Museum closed for renovation  
**H)** Advice on reducing screen time  

**1.** The clinic offers blood tests and blood pressure checks every Tuesday. No appointment needed for basic screening.

**2.** Turn off notifications and keep phones out of the bedroom. Doctors say this improves sleep quality.

**3.** Duolingo and similar apps let you practise vocabulary for ten minutes a day. Premium versions remove advertisements.

**4.** Sort plastic, paper, and glass into separate bins. The city collects recyclables every Wednesday morning.

**5.** The Riverside Stage hosts jazz and folk bands from June to August. Bring a blanket and enjoy the open air.

**6.** The programme includes weekly long runs and nutrition workshops. Registration closes on 15 April.

## Task 2 — Multiple Choice

### Text: Public Transport

City officials plan to introduce new electric buses next year. The vehicles produce less noise and pollution than diesel models. Tickets will cost the same, but passengers may need a contactless card instead of cash. Critics argue that bus lanes should be expanded first; supporters say cleaner air is worth the investment.

**7.** What is the text mainly about?
- A) Building new airports
- B) Improving urban bus services
- C) Banning all public transport
- D) Teaching people to drive

**8.** Electric buses are described as ______ than diesel ones.
- A) louder and dirtier
- B) quieter and cleaner
- C) slower and cheaper
- D) older and safer

**9.** Passengers might need to ______.
- A) pay only in cash
- B) use a contactless card
- C) walk instead of taking a bus
- D) buy their own bus

**10.** Critics think the city should first ______.
- A) expand bus lanes
- B) remove all tickets
- C) stop electric buses
- D) close the centre

**11.** The word **«supporters»** refers to people who ______.
- A) oppose the plan
- B) agree with the changes
- C) work as bus drivers only
- D) never use buses
