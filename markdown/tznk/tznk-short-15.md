# ТЗНК Short Practice — 15 questions (Day 28)

**Time:** 35 minutes | Use after Mock English #2

## Part A — Logic & Verbal (11 questions)

**1.** DOCTOR : HOSPITAL :: TEACHER : ______
- A) book
- B) school
- C) chalk
- D) student

**2.** Which word is the odd one out?
- A) violin
- B) trumpet
- C) novel
- D) drum

**3.** All mammals breathe air. Whales are mammals. What follows?
- A) Whales do not breathe air
- B) Whales breathe air
- C) No mammals live in water
- D) All animals are whales

**4.** If 3x = 18, then x = ?
- A) 3
- B) 6
- C) 9
- D) 15

**5.** « Reluctant » most nearly means ______
- A) eager
- B) unwilling
- C) happy
- D) loud

**6.** Begin : End :: Open : ______
- A) close
- B) start
- C) door
- D) key

**7.** A meeting starts at 14:20 and lasts 50 minutes. It ends at ______
- A) 15:00
- B) 15:10
- C) 15:20
- D) 14:70

**8.** Statement: «Some students passed. All who passed received certificates.» Which is true?
- A) No students received certificates
- B) Some students received certificates
- C) All students failed
- D) Certificates were not given

**9.** Antonym of « ancient »:
- A) old
- B) modern
- C) historic
- D) broken

**10.** Sequence: 3, 6, 12, 24, ___
- A) 30
- B) 36
- C) 48
- D) 27

**11.** Choose the best conclusion: «Rain cancelled the outdoor exam. The exam was moved online.»
- A) The exam was cancelled permanently
- B) Weather caused a change of format
- C) Students refused to take the exam
- D) Online exams are always illegal
