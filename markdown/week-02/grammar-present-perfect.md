# Grammar — Present Perfect vs Past Simple

Murphy **Intermediate** Units 1–2 | **Elementary** Units 7–8

---

## Rule summary (for student — Ukrainian)

| Past Simple | Present Perfect |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Завершена дія в минулому | Зв'язок із теперішнім |
| yesterday, last week, in 2019 | for, since, ever, never, just, already, yet |
| I **visited** Paris in 2019. | I **have visited** Paris three times. |

---

## Drill 1 — Choose the correct form

1. She ______ (graduated / has graduated) last June.
2. I ______ (know / have known) him since childhood.
3. ______ you ever ______ (be / been) to Edinburgh?
4. They ______ ( didn't finish / haven't finished) the report yet.
5. He ______ (just / yesterday) ______ (left / has left) the office.
6. We ______ (lived / have lived) here for five years.
7. Shakespeare ______ (wrote / has written) many plays. (historical fact → PS)
8. ______ (Did you see / Have you seen) the email I sent this morning?

**Key:** 1-graduated  2-have known  3-Have/been  4-haven't finished  5-has just left  6-have lived  7-wrote  8-Have you seen

## Drill 2 — Gapped text (exam style)

Many students (1)______ abroad at least once during their bachelor's degree. In 2022, Olena (2)______ a semester in Poland and she (3)______ Polish friends who she still contacts. Since then, she (4)______ to three other countries. «I (5)______ never ______ such independence before,» she says.

| Gap | A | B | C | D |
|-----|---|---|---|---|
| (1) | study | studied | have studied | are studying |
| (2) | spends | spent | has spent | is spending |
| (3) | makes | made | has made | is making |
| (4) | travels | travelled | has travelled | is travelling |
| (5) | did / feel | do / feel | have / felt | will / feel |

**Key:** 1-C  2-B  3-B  4-C  5-C

---

## Reading tie-in (Task 2 skill)

When you see **since**, **for**, **ever**, **yet**, **already** in questions — check tense in text carefully. Wrong tense in answer options is a common trap.
