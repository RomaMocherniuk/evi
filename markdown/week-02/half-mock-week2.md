## Task 2 — Multiple Choice

### Text: Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence is changing how students study. Some use chatbots to check grammar or summarise long articles. Educators worry that learners may rely on technology instead of developing their own skills. Several universities have updated their rules: AI can be used for brainstorming, but final essays must be the student's own work.

Researchers say AI will not replace teachers soon, but it may change what happens in the classroom. Students might spend less time on routine tasks and more on discussion and problem-solving. The challenge is to use new tools responsibly.
