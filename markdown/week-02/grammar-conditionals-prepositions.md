## Conditionals

### Zero Conditional (general truths)
**If + Present Simple, Present Simple**  
If you heat ice, it melts.

### First Conditional (real future)
**If + Present Simple, will + infinitive**  
If I pass the ЄВІ, I will apply to KNU.

### Common exam traps
- If it **will** rain... ❌ → If it **rains**... ✓
- Second conditional (would) — Week 4

### Gap drill

1. If you ______ (don't sleep / won't sleep) enough, you feel tired.
2. If she ______ (passes / will pass) the exam, she ______ celebrate.
3. Water boils if you ______ (heat / will heat) it to 100°C.
4. If they ______ ( arrive / will arrive) late, they ______ miss the instructions.

**Key:** 1-don't sleep  2-passes / will  3-heat  4-arrive / will

## Comparatives & Superlatives

| Rule | Example |
|------|---------|
| short adj + -er | cheaper |
| long adj: more + adj | more expensive |
| irregular | good/better/best; bad/worse/worst |
| as...as | She is as tall as her brother. |

**Gap:** This task is ______ (difficult) than the previous one. → **more difficult**

## Prepositions

### Time
- **in** May, 2024, the morning
- **on** Monday, 5 May
- **at** 3 p.m., night, the weekend (UK)

### Place
- **in** Ukraine, the room
- **at** the station, home
- **on** the wall, the bus

### Movement
- **to** school | **into** the building | **from** Kyiv **to** Lviv

### Dependent prepositions (lexis!)
- depend **on**, interested **in**, good **at**, afraid **of**, apply **for**

### Gap drill (5–10)

5. The exam is ______ 15 July ______ 9 a.m.
6. She is interested ______ environmental science.
7. This book is ______ (useful) than the old edition.
8. He arrived ______ the airport two hours early.
9. It depends ______ the weather.
10. This is the ______ (good) result she has ever had.

**Key:** 5-on/at  6-in  7-more useful  8-at  9-on  10-best
