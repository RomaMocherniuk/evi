## Murphy Unit Map (Tier 1–2)

### Elementary (Weeks 1–2)
- Units 1–2: Present tenses
- Units 5–6: Past Simple / Continuous
- Units 7–8: Present Perfect
- Units 9–10: Have/has, there is/are
- Units 11–12: Modals
- Units 13–15: Articles, this/that
- Units 26–27: Comparatives, superlatives
- Units 30–31: Prepositions time/place

### Intermediate (Weeks 3–4)
- Units 1–2: Present Perfect vs Past Simple
- Units 3–4: Passive
- Units 5–6: Conditionals
- Units 19–20: Relative clauses
- Units 38–39: Reported speech (basics)
- Units 53–54: Verb + -ing / to
